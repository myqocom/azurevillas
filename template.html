Parisian Prestige, Redefined

Just steps from luxury shopping and iconic landmarks, experience modern elegance and refined Parisian glamour.

Rooms & Suites

Bellevoire Champs-Élysées offers 22 meticulously styled rooms and suites, blending modern Parisian design with comfort and class.

8 Deluxe Rooms

Featuring soft neutral palettes, plush king beds, and large windows overlooking the iconic Parisian boulevards.

6 Avenue View Rooms

Elegant rooms with direct views of the Champs-Élysées and custom furniture inspired by mid-century Paris.

5 Bellevoire Prestige Suites

Spacious, light-filled suites with private balconies, premium furnishings, and designer lighting.

3 Grand Parisian Residences

Ultimate indulgence — expansive suites with dining areas, soaking tubs, curated art, and full panoramic city views.

Each room includes:

  • High-thread-count linens & premium mattresses

  • Marble-clad bathrooms with rain showers or tubs

  • Soundproof windows

  • In-room espresso bar

  • Complimentary Wi-Fi and entertainment system

  • Handcrafted minibar selection

  • Signature Bellevoire fragrance amenities

Amenities

Elevated Comfort in Every Detail

  • Private Rooftop Lounge: Sunset cocktails with views of the Eiffel Tower

  • In-Room Dining: Refined French cuisine brought to your door

  • Wellness Studio: Personal training, yoga, and massage services

  • Concierge Services: From private drivers to exclusive shopping appointments

  • Petite Bellevoire Café: A stylish breakfast and afternoon tea lounge

  • VIP Transfers: Luxury car service to/from Charles de Gaulle or Le Bourget

Location

Located on the legendary Champs-Élysées, the hotel places you moments away from the Arc de Triomphe, Place de la Concorde, and the finest haute couture fashion houses in Paris.

Bellevoire, Reimagined

At Bellevoire Champs-Élysées, luxury isn’t loud — it’s curated, personal, and deeply Parisian. Whether you’re in town for fashion, business, or romance, our boutique hotel offers a stay that’s both memorable and meaningful.
